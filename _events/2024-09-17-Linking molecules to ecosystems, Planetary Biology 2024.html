Join the Planetary Biology conference in Gothenburg, 17-18 September with the aim to create opportunities to integrate cross-scale methodological approaches to advance our understanding of ecosystem interactions and functions.

The Planetary Biology Capability at SciLifeLab announce its first conference and all-hands meeting on the theme “Linking molecules to ecosystems.” The main goal of this conference is to create opportunities to integrate cross-scale methodological approaches to advance our understanding of ecosystem interactions and functions.

Day one includes inspirational talks, poster presentations, mingling, and two interactive sessions to:

1) Foster opportunities to develop collaborations.

2) Overcome challenges in applying new technologies and cross-scale approaches to span from molecules to ecosystems.

  • Free of charge conference!
  • When? September 17-18, 2024 in Gothenburg
  • Deadline to submit abstracts: 16h August
  • Deadline for registration: 1st September
