The Swedish Oikos Conference 2025 will be held in Stockholm. Save the date.

This will be the 42nd conference of the Swedish Oikos Society, and the sixth time it is hosted in Stockholm. The conference will gather researchers and students from across Sweden to share ideas and discoveries in ecological research, and solutions for our changing world. We will cover a diverse range of topics, ranging from genes to ecosystems, in Sweden and beyond.

The conference will take place on the 4th and 5th of February, 2025, at the Swedish Museum of Natural History. It is also arranged an icebreaker on Monday the 3rd, please save the date for this as well.
