The inauguration of the Swedish Biodiversity Data Infrastructure, a platform for publishing and accessing data on biodiversity, was recorded and is now available. During the launch we introduced Sweden’s most important e-infrastructure for biodiversity and ecosystem research,

The official inauguration of Sweden’s national access hub for biodiversity data, the Swedish Biodiversity Data Infrastructure (SBDI) took place Monday October 4, 2021.

See the full inauguration here.

Key note speakers during the inauguration ceremony were Alexandre Antonelli, Director of Science, Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew, and Donald Hobern, Executive Secretary, International Consortium for the Barcoding of Life (iBOL). SBDI was officially inaugurated by Maria Thuveson, Executive Director at the Swedish Research Council and Head of the Swedish GBIF Delegation. 

The inauguration ceremony was followed by three webinars showcasing key functionality for biodiversity and ecosystems research. These webinars will soon be available to watch.
