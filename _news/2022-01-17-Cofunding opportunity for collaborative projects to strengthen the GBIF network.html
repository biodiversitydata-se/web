--- title: "Cofunding opportunity for collaborative projects to strengthen the GBIF network" date: 2022-01-17 image: ---

GBIF invites current GBIF participants (both voting and associate) to submit concept notes for project funding through the Capacity Enhancement Support Programme (CESP), which enables collaboration between GBIF participants to address identified needs at regional and global levels.

Concept notes for this cofunding opportunity for collaborative projects that target capacity enhancement and exchange to strengthen the GBIF networkmust be submitted by 1 February 2022. More information at the GBIF site.
