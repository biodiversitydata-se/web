PLAN-SUBSIM delivers first object detection models for benthic fauna and habitat builders.

PLatform for ANalysis of SUBSea IMage aims at leverage existing methods, knowledge, and infrastructure in the field of subsea image analysis and implement these for applications in marine resource management. The initial use cases of PLAN-SUBSIM focus on images of biota and biological habitats.

Learn more by taking part of the report describing the first object detection models for benthic fauna and habitat builders.
