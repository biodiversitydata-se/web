Do you have already collected soil biodiversity data?

Then you can seek grant mobilization of your data. The call solicits applications by researchers/research groups from the group of SoilBON collaborators, for projects and processes related to digital data mobilization of already collected soil biodiversity data. Soil biodiversity data holders not currently affiliated to SoilBON may contact the email below for available options to join the network.

Submission deadline: Sunday, 10 July 2022.
