During 2022 and the start of 2023, SMHI has published four new datasets from the SHARK database.

It concerns time series from national and regional monitoring programs for vegetated bottoms (Epibenthos), as well as time series from national programs for ringed seal (Pusa hispida) and picoplankton. Previously published time series have all been updated with new data in 2022. All datasets are sampling event data and contain a lot of information in addition to species observations.
