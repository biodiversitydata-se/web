The DTO-BioFlow project announce an open call for marine biodiversity data holders who can contribute to the development by providing sustained and long-term biodiversity data for the EU DTO.

They are looking for international networks, citizen science networks, research institutes, universities, NGOs, etc working within marine biodiversity domain, interested in sharing their data to establish ingestion pipelines and to increase the flow of relevant biodiversity data to the EU DTOmarine.

Each applicants can access a maximum funding amount of 60,000 EUR.

An opportunity to shape the future of marine data management! Applications close on January 17th 2024.

Read more on website and apply.
