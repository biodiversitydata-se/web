Tuesday, 9th April 2024, 09.00-14.30 is the official launch of the Swedish national platform for subsea image analysis (SUBSIM).

SUBSIM supports research and monitoring in marine ecosystems with open-source services for subsea image analysis. The platform is part of Swedish Biodiversity Data Infrastructure (SBDI).
